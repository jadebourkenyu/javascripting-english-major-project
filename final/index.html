Book Summary

Open City appears to be a plotless book about a Nigerian doctor, Julius. Julius lives near Morningside Park and frequently goes on walks throughout the city. During these walks, he reminisces on his past and thinks in depth about the knowledge he has on various topics like history, art and music. Juilius has, more often than not, negative experiences with other people which unfolds to be a result of his poor attitude and actions. He travels abroad to Brussels towards the middle of the novel.
